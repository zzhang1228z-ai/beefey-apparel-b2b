Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about importing custom pajamas directly from our factory.

Q: Can you manufacture sleepwear according to custom sizing charts?

A: Yes! We can develop custom pajama patterns according to your specific US, European, or Australian sizing guides. We can also provide standard commercial size templates for your selection.

Q: Do you offer free samples before starting bulk production?

A: We provide free fabric swatch books with our core colors. For physical pre-production garment samples, a sample development fee is charged, which is 100% fully refunded upon your bulk production order.

Q: How does your factory handle quality control?

A: We implement strict inspection protocols: fabric pre-shrunk testing, inline sewing quality reviews, 100% inspection before packing, and final metal detection. Our standards fully align with AQL 2.5 benchmarks.
