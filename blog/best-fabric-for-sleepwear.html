B2B Sourcing Guide

Start with the handfeel your customer expects.

For premium softness and breathability, bamboo viscose and modal are strong choices. For polished pajama sets, satin and lyocell create a smoother drape. Cotton and gauze work well for natural, breathable collections. Beefey supports fabric recommendation, color matching, custom prints, sampling and bulk sleepwear production.

Fabric Library

Main sleepwear fabrics we develop

Each fabric can be customized by composition, weight, handfeel, color, print and finishing method.

☘

Bamboo Viscose

A key fabric direction for Beefey. Bamboo viscose has a soft, cool touch and smooth drape, making it suitable for premium pajamas, robes and loungewear.

  • Cool touch
  • Breathable
  • Fluid drape
≈

Modal

Modal is smooth, soft and comfortable against the skin. It is a strong option for modern pajama sets, lounge tops and relaxed sleepwear collections.

  • Silky soft
  • Comfort stretch
  • Elegant drape
◆

Satin / Silky Fabric

Satin brings a smooth surface and refined shine for elegant pajama sets, bridal robes, gift collections and seasonal sleepwear lines.

  • Smooth sheen
  • Print ready
  • Gift set feel
◌

Lyocell

Lyocell has a clean, smooth handfeel with a natural drape. It works well for brands looking for a premium cellulose-based sleepwear fabric.

  • Cool handfeel
  • Smooth surface
  • Premium drape
✦

Cotton

Cotton is familiar, breathable and retail friendly. It is suitable for everyday pajama programs, family sets, basics and printed sleepwear collections.

  • Natural feel
  • Easy care
  • Retail friendly
▧

Gauze

Gauze fabric is lightweight, airy and softly textured. It is a good choice for breathable summer sleepwear, relaxed robes and natural lifestyle collections.

  • Airy weave
  • Soft crinkle
  • Lightweight
✿

Custom Printed Fabric

From floral prints to seasonal graphics, we support artwork development and repeat-print production across bamboo, modal, cotton, satin and other bases.

  • Custom artwork
  • Repeat prints
