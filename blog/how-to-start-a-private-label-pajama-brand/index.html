Quick buyer questions

Short answers for brands comparing private label pajama manufacturers.

What is the MOQ for private label pajamas?

Many standard programs can start from 100 sets, depending on fabric, color, size range and customization details.

Can I use my own logo and packaging?

Yes. Beefey supports custom neck labels, wash labels, hangtags, logo packaging, gift boxes and retail-ready presentation.

Which fabric is best for a new pajama brand?

Satin is strong for bridal and gifting. Bamboo and modal are strong for premium comfort. Cotton is classic for everyday sleepwear.

Can Beefey help if I only have reference images?

Yes. Send your references, target market and quantity. The team can recommend suitable styles, fabrics and sample options.
