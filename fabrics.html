Bamboo, Modal & Satin Sleepwear Fabrics

Fabric credibility is a key trust signal for premium sleepwear buyers. Beefey highlights handfeel, GSM, shrinkage and pilling considerations early in the sourcing process.

Fabric Development

Material options built around comfort and repeat purchase.

Bamboo viscose, modal and satin sleepwear are high-interest categories for buyers seeking soft handfeel, elevated presentation and better customer retention. Beefey can support custom colors, GSM selection, trims, labels and packaging based on each buyer’s positioning.

For higher-control programs, project targets can include shrinkage control within ±3%, anti-pilling grade 4 and third-party test support through SGS, ITS or buyer-nominated labs.

Bamboo Viscose

Breathable, naturally soft and suitable for elevated lounge sets, premium basics and next-to-skin sleepwear.

Modal Blend

Smooth drape, a fluid handfeel and clean surface texture that works well for premium pajama programs.

Satin

Soft sheen, visible luster and refined piping compatibility for bridal, gifting and premium retail sleepwear.

Soft Jersey

Stretch recovery and soft jersey touch for comfort-led sleepwear, nursing styles and cozy everyday programs.

Lace & Trims
